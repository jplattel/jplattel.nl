Generating Mondrian like art with D3 & Javascript

05-03-2013

SETUP is holding a competition to get programming known as modern day craftwork. I whole-fully agree to this and decided to enter the contest!

With a small part of my time spend while traveling I and finally finishing up I’ve gotten my last version up and running. I use javascript and D3 (SVG) for drawing the elements as this is a library that I wanted to explore. The ease of use and adoption rate of it made a good starting point for me to quickly get to a result.
